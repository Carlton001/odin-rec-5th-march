Spaghetti Recipe


Description

this is a delicious plate of spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 pack spaghetti
  • 3 fresh tomatoes (blended)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water for 8–10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato paste and blended tomatoes.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes until thick.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Mix sauce with spaghetti or serve on top.
