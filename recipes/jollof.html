Jollof Recipe


Description

this is a tasty plate of ghana jollof

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 3 blended tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or beef (optional)


Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions.
  2. Add tomato paste and blended tomatoes. Cook for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Add curry, thyme, salt, and stock.
  4. Pour in washed rice and stir.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for about 25 minutes.
  6. Stir occasionally until the rice is soft.
