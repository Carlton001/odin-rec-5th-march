Fried Rice Recipe


Description

this is a sweet delicacy of fried rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken pieces (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Fry onions until soft.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Push vegetables aside and scramble the eggs.
  5. Add rice and soy sauce.
  6. Stir everything together and cook for 5 minutes.
