Pancake Recipe


Description

this is a perfectly made pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp melted butter or oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add egg and milk and mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and lightly oil it.
  4. Pour small batter into the pan.
  5. Cook 2–3 minutes each side until golden.
